Telemetry / Video integration

If you have video and telemetry files Vbo tools can help you perform full Video analysis in Circuit Tools. Just like having the Video Vbox Hardware! You just need to integrate the video and telemetry files using Vbo tools.
Watch a video tutorial and learn how to use VboTools!
If you have .dbn files instead of .vbo, use vbotools to convert accordingly.
